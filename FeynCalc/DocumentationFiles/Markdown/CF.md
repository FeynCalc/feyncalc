##  CF 

CF is one of the Casimir operator eigenvalues of SU(N) (CF = ($N^2$-1)/(2 N))..

###  See also 

CA, SUNSimplify.

###  Examples 

```mathematica
CF 
 
SUNSimplify[CF, SUNNToCACF -> False] 
 
SUNN 
 
SUNSimplify[SUNN^2 - 1, SUNNToCACF -> True]
```
